# Cohesion

**Keep your mods in sync with the servers you play on — automatically.**

Ever joined a Minecraft server only to be told you're missing mods, have the wrong versions, or need to remove conflicting ones? Cohesion takes care of all that for you.

## How it works

When you connect to a server running Cohesion, the mod checks if your installed mods match what the server expects. If something's off, you'll see a clear summary of what needs to change — which mods to install, update, or remove — and you choose whether to apply the sync or disconnect.

Hit **Sync & Restart**, and Cohesion handles everything: downloading the right versions, removing what's no longer needed, and getting your game ready. Just relaunch Minecraft and you're good to go.

## Switching between servers

Play on multiple servers? No problem. Each time you connect, Cohesion adjusts your mods to match that server's setup. Mods are cached locally, so switching back to a server you've played on before is instant — nothing gets re-downloaded.

## Your mods, your control

- **You always choose.** Cohesion never modifies your mods without showing you exactly what will change and asking for confirmation.
- **Your personal mods are safe.** Cohesion only manages mods it installed. Anything you added yourself stays untouched.
- **Essential mods are protected.** Fabric API, Fabric Loader, and Cohesion itself are never removed, no matter what.

## For server owners

Add Cohesion to your server and define which mods your players need in a simple config file. When players connect, they'll be prompted to sync — no more troubleshooting broken mod lists or writing setup guides. Players without Cohesion can still join normally.
